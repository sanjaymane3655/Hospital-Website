Our Speciality's

Allergy

An allergist or immunologist focuses on preventing and treating allergic diseases and conditions. These usually include various types of allergies and asthma.

Dermatology

Dermatologists focus on diseases and conditions of the skin, nails, and hair. They treat conditions such as eczema, skin cancer, acne, and psoriasis.

Cardiology

Cardiologists focus on the cardiovascular system, which includes the heart and blood vessels. They treat many cardiovascular conditions, such as:high blood pressure, high cholesterol.

nephrology

A nephrologist focuses on kidney care and conditions that affect the kidneys.
